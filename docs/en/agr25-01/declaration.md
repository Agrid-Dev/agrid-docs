## Applicable Directives & Standards

### Radio Equipment Directive (RED)

**Directive:** 2014/53/EU - Radio Equipment Directive

The AGR25-01 incorporates radio transmission capability (WiFi 2.4GHz) and complies with all essential requirements of the RED, including:

- Safety: Protection against electrical hazards and thermal effects
- Health: Electromagnetic exposure limits per EN 62311
- Spectrum: Radio frequency management per EN 300 328
- Electromagnetic Compatibility: EN 301 489-1 and EN 301 489-17

### RoHS Directive

**Directive:** 2011/65/EU - Restriction of Hazardous Substances

The AGR25-01 contains no lead (Pb), mercury (Hg), cadmium (Cd), hexavalent chromium (Cr VI), polybrominated biphenyls (PBB), or polybrominated diphenyl ethers (PBDE) above the exemption thresholds.

### WEEE Directive

**Directive:** 2012/19/EU - Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment

The WEEE logo on this product indicates that at end-of-life, the thermostat must be collected separately and returned to an authorized waste management facility. See the Installation Guide for disposal instructions.

### Ecodesign Regulation

**Regulation:** 2024/1103 - Ecodesign Requirements for Energy-Related Products

The AGR25-01 meets all ecodesign requirements for independent connected controls including:

- Maximum power consumption limits
- Standby power consumption optimization
- Availability of product information (per Annex VI)
- Product fiche data provided (see Ecodesign Information section)

## Restrictions on Use

The AGR25-01 is intended for use as an automatic temperature control device in HVAC systems. It is designed for:

- **Indoor installation only**
- **Dry environments** (IP20 rating)
- **Ambient temperatures** 5°C to 40°C
- **Mains supply voltage** 220-240V~ 50Hz

The product is not suitable for:

- Wet or damp locations
- Extreme temperatures (below 0°C or above 50°C)
- Outdoor installations
- Life-supporting or safety-critical systems
- Direct control of high-power loads (external contactor required for heaters)
